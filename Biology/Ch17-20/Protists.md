- All Eukaryotes
- Damp or aquatic environments
- Organized by nutrition type

## Protozoans - animal like - heterotrophs

- Organized by type of movement

### Ciliophora
- Cilia to move
- Eats bacteria
- Has contractile vacuole
- Binary fission, conjugation
